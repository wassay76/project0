ALL ABOUT GOD OF WAR

God of War is an action-adventure game franchise created
by David Jaffe at Sony's Santa Monica Studio.It began in 2005 on
the PlayStation 2 video game console, and has become a flagship
title for the PlayStation brand,consisting of eight games across
multiple platforms
