ALL ABOUT MODERNWARFARE

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a 2019 first-person shooter
video game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision.
Serving as the sixteenth overall installment in the Call of Duty
series, as well as a reboot of the Modern Warfare sub-series,[1][2][3]
it was released on October 25, 2019, for Microsoft Windows,
PlayStation 4, and Xbox One.
